  2. DURATION — This Agreement commences on the date of delivery of the Vehicle and remains in force until the agreed expiry date.
  3. OWNERSHIP
    1. The Rentor remains the owner and may inspect the Vehicle at reasonable times. The Rentee must notify the Rentor of any change of address.
    2. The Rentee shall comply with Road Traffic Laws and shall not use the Vehicle for unauthorised commercial purposes, abuse or modify the Vehicle, drive negligently, carry damaging materials, drive under the influence, enter unrest areas, permit unauthorised drivers, or take the Vehicle outside Victoria without prior written consent.
    3. The Rentee must safeguard the Vehicle, pay for consumables, allow access on request, lock the Vehicle and retain the keys when unattended, and activate immobiliser/alarm systems.
  4. VEHICLE MAINTENANCE — The Rentee must maintain the Vehicle in roadworthy condition, not tamper with tracking/immobiliser, service per manufacturer at approved dealers at Rentee’s cost, and pay $500 admin if failure to service causes plan termination.
  5. LIMITATION ON TRAVEL — 3000 km per week maximum; $0.70 per km excess. Odometer is authoritative unless malfunction reported immediately; tracking may be used to verify. Excess is calculated on expiry or early termination and payable on return.
  6. COLLISION DAMAGE, THEFT OR TOTAL LOSS — Rentee must notify Rentor and police within 24 hours, not admit liability, use authorised towing, and is liable for amounts beyond waivers. If repairs exceed value or fair value is reduced, Rentee pays the shortfall. Rentor may provide a replacement vehicle at its discretion or Rentee may elect early termination once Rentor is compensated.
  7. FINES AND PENALTIES — Rentee pays fines and tolls plus $10 admin each. Accumulation of 12+ offenses in 12 months is a material breach allowing termination.
  8. DAMAGE AND LOSS LIABILITY WAIVER — Liability limited by waivers; windscreens/tyres may be excluded and for Rentee’s account; repudiated claims due to Rentee’s breach remain Rentee’s liability.
  9. RETURN OF THE VEHICLE — Rentee must return Vehicle with all keys, accessories and books; deposits returned after inspection; repairs/replacements may be set off; late return incurs additional rental plus 20% penalty; no transfer of ownership or obligation to sell.
  10. AREA OF USE — Vehicle must remain within the state of registration; use on sealed bitumen roads only; breach voids waivers.
  11. INDEMNITIES — Rentee indemnifies Rentor for fines and third-party claims not covered by insurance.
  12. GENERAL — Entire agreement; variations only in signed writing; no estoppel from indulgences; no external representations bind the parties.
  13. CONSENT TO JURISDICTION — Non-exclusive jurisdiction of Victoria applies.
